Three Fruit Choices

Product Flavors

Simply Beverages offers three refreshing fruit flavors designed to provide a fun and enjoyable drinking experience. Each flavor has its own unique personality while remaining part of the overall Simply brand.

Strawberry, Lemon, and Orange

Bright colors, fruit illustrations, and carefully selected design elements help communicate the taste and energy of each beverage. The Strawberry flavor features a vibrant red color palette that represents sweetness and freshness. The label design incorporates playful strawberry illustrations that immediately communicate the flavor to consumers. This flavor is designed for people who enjoy a fruity and refreshing beverage with a naturally sweet taste.

The Lemon flavor uses bright yellow tones that create a clean and energetic appearance. Lemon is often associated with freshness and vitality, making it an ideal choice for consumers looking for a crisp and refreshing drink. The packaging reflects these qualities through simple yet eye-catching design elements. The yellow bottle also creates a strong contrast against the red and orange designs, helping the full product line feel energetic on display.

The Orange flavor combines warm orange colors with fun fruit graphics to create a bold and inviting look. This flavor is designed to appeal to consumers who enjoy classic citrus beverages with a refreshing twist. The orange design feels cheerful and familiar, while the character artwork gives it the same playful brand voice as the other bottles.

Flavor Goals

  • Use color to make each flavor easy to recognize.
  • Keep the logo and label structure consistent across the product line.
  • Create a cheerful drink family that feels fresh, simple, and approachable.

Each flavor was carefully designed to maintain consistency across the product line while allowing individual flavors to stand out. Together, these beverages create a cohesive product family that reflects the core values of the Simply brand: freshness, simplicity, and enjoyment.
