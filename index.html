Simply Beverages

Welcome to Simply Beverages, a refreshing beverage brand created to bring natural fruit flavors and simple ingredients together in a fun and modern way. Our brand was developed with the idea that great-tasting drinks do not need to be complicated.

Fresh Design Beyond the Bottle

By combining bright fruit flavors with clean and attractive packaging, Simply Beverages offers a product that appeals to people looking for a refreshing non-alcoholic drink. The Simply brand was created as a packaging and branding design project that demonstrates the complete design process from concept development to final product presentation.

Every element of the brand was carefully designed to communicate freshness, simplicity, and quality. The colorful fruit illustrations and vibrant labels help each flavor stand out while maintaining a consistent visual identity. The logo, bottle colors, label artwork, and promotional materials work together to make the brand memorable on a shelf, in an advertisement, and online.

Our goal is to create a memorable brand experience that connects with consumers and showcases professional design skills. This website highlights the development of the Simply brand, including logo creation, flavor packaging, product renderings, and promotional materials. Visitors can explore the design decisions behind the project and see how branding can transform a simple beverage into a recognizable product.

Whether you are interested in graphic design, branding, or product packaging, Simply Beverages demonstrates how thoughtful design can create a strong and engaging visual identity that appeals to a modern audience. The vehicle wrap extends the same pink fruit pattern, logo, bottle graphics, and character illustrations onto a promotional car so the brand can be seen in motion. The project also shows how color, type, character illustration, and product photography can support one clear brand message: fresh juice should feel simple, bright, and enjoyable.
