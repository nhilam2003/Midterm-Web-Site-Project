Brand System

Packaging Design

Packaging plays an important role in how consumers perceive a product. For the Simply Beverages project, the packaging was designed to create a strong visual identity while clearly communicating important product information.

Balancing Creativity and Function

The goal was to balance creativity with functionality, ensuring that the packaging was both attractive and informative. The design process began with logo development and concept sketches. Multiple ideas were explored before selecting the final logo, which features a bold and simple style that is easy to recognize. The logo serves as the foundation for the entire brand identity and appears consistently across all product variations.

Each label includes essential packaging components such as nutritional information, ingredients, product descriptions, and barcodes. These elements were organized carefully to maintain readability without overwhelming the overall design. Bright colors and fruit illustrations were used to differentiate flavors while supporting the brand's playful personality. The front of each label quickly identifies the flavor, while the side and back panels support practical information a customer would expect to find.

Product renderings were created to showcase how the final beverages would appear on store shelves. Highlights, shadows, and realistic bottle effects were added to create a professional presentation. These renderings help demonstrate how the packaging functions in a real-world environment and allow viewers to visualize the finished product from multiple angles.

The final packaging successfully combines branding, information, and visual appeal into a cohesive design system that supports the Simply Beverages identity. It also shows how a student design project can move beyond a single flat image and become a complete product presentation.
